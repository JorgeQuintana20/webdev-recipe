How to Make Mozzarella Sticks



Ingredients

  • 16 oz block whole milk cheese
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2/3 cup italian style breadcrumbs
  • salt
  • freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Cut cheese into sticks
  2. Take cheese sticks and dip into egg
  3. Coat the sticks with breadcrumbs
  4. Heat up a fryer to 365 degrees F, and fry for about 30-60 seconds
